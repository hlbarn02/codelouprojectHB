Hello! My name is Hanna and I'm an artist,adventurer,wife, and a jewelry designer amongst other things. I have a huge love for all things shiny and sparkly and I love to use natural elements like leather and stones.I love fairy tales and magic and I try to put a little whimsy and magic in each peice I create. I like to imagine each peice taking a grand adventure with whomever buys it. I am a self-taught jewelry designer and enjoy working with many mediums, although leather and semi-precious gemstones are definitely my favorite. Many of my designs are completely unique and one of a kind. I'm inspired by a combination of simplicity, nature, fairy tales, and Bohemian style. I am constantly thinking of new ideas and designs wherever I am. I also have a business background and a love of learning and I am always looking for new ways to perfect my skills. I love making unique peices for people and seeing the expression on their face when they see it for the first time. The process of creating truly brings me satisfaction and joy. My jewelry line, pangofineart®, is currently available at local craft/art shows and will be available on Etsy soon. Feel free to contact me if you have a special piece in mind!!
